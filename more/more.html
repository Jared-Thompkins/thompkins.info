Harvard's Summer Venture in Management Program (SVMP)

Harvard's Summer Venture in Management Program is a unique and intensive week-long residential program that takes place in the historic city of Cambridge, Massachusetts. This immersive experience is designed to provide individuals with insights into the life and career advantages that come with an HBS MBA. Essay questions include:

  1. Outside of school, what do you like to do? What are your hobbies and interests?
  2. What is your dream job?
  3. Who do you admire most?
